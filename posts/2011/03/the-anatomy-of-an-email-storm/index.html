The Anatomy of an Email Storm

March 10, 2011 One-minute read
Email • Fun

The Wall Street Journal devoted a full article this week to the Reply-All button. Towards the end, they brought up one of Reply-All’s unintended consequences: the email storm. This is where someone accidentally emails a very large distribution list, people then Reply-All asking to be removed, then the whole thing explodes until IT gets involved.
